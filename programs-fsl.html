WHAT WE DO

Building incomes that outlast the crisis.

Emergency food keeps a family alive today. Savings groups, microloans and vocational training make sure they don't need emergency help again tomorrow. Our FSL programmes do both — together.

Our interventions include:

  • Emergency food distribution — reaching the most food-insecure families first.
  • Savings groups & microfinance — small loans and savings circles that build capital.
  • Vocational & business training — practical skills that turn into steady income.
  • Climate-smart farming support — helping farmers adapt to a changing climate.
